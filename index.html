Our Pride

National Anthem

All hail,Liberia,hail!(All hail)
This glorious land of liberity
shall long be ours.

Though new her name,
Green been her frame,[a]
And mighty be her powers,

Though new her name,
Green been her frame,

And mighty be her powers
And mighty be her powers,

In joy and glandness
With our heart united,
We'll shout the freedom
Of a race benighted,

Long live liberia,happy land!
A home of glorious liberity
by God's command!

All hail, Liberia, hail! (All hail!)
In union strong success is sure
We cannot fail!

With God above
our right to prove
We will o'er previal,
We will o'er previal,

With heart and hand
Our country's cause defending
We'll meet the foe
With valor unpretending.

Long live Liberia, happy land!
A home of glorious liberity,
By God's command!
